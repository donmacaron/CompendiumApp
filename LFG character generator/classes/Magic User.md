# Magic User Class Guide

## Overview

> "You are a magic user, a student of strange and supernatural powers that are not well understood. You might be a mysterious conjuror, a dread witchdoctor, or an eccentric sorcerer with a six demon bag."

**Core Philosophy:**
- Magic is most multi-faceted power in game
- Can conjure lightning, invoke spirits, beguile onlookers, mend wounds
- There is price to be paid; no spell uttered lightly
- Knows value of sharp blade and steady arm

### Key Attributes
- **Primary:** Intelligence

### Hit Points
| Level Range | HP Formula | HP Bonus |
|-------------|------------|----------|
| Up to 9th | 1d3 + 3 (+Con if any) per level | — |
| 10th+ | 1 hp/level | — |

### Equipment
- **Armour:** Light only
- **Shields:** Not allowed
- **Weapons:** One-handed weapons, light crossbows, sling

### Skills (Choose at character creation)
- **Proficient:** Arcane Lore, Insight
- **Additional (+4):** Animal Lore, Athletics, Apothecary, Deception, Detection, Divine Lore, Gather Information, General Lore, Persuasion, Sailing, Stealth, Wilderness Lore

---

## Spell Casting System (1st Level)

### Learning Spells
- At 1st level: Know **one spell** + bonus number of spells equal to Int modifier
- Choose bonus spells from 1st level spell list
- Each level thereafter: Learn number of spells = Int modifier
- Can choose from highest known spell level per advancement table

### Additional Spell Sources
- Finding scrolls/spell books: May learn one extra spell when advancing a level

### Substitution
Once per level: May substitute one known spell for another spell

### Elemental Variants
When learning spells that unleash/affect specific elements, may learn elemental variant instead (e.g., Gift of Fiery Furnace → Gift of Frozen Vortex)

---

## Spell Uses & Casting

### Usage at Start of Adventure
Per advancement table: May use number of spells per spell level (see table below)

### Regaining Uses
**Regain:** Short/long rest (p.81)
- Choose which known spells to cast at time of casting

---

## Important Casting Rules

| Rule | Detail |
|------|--------|
| Concentration required | Must concentrate when casting |
| Damage restriction | If suffers any damage BEFORE your turn, cannot cast spell that round |
| Choice timing | Choose which spells to cast at time of casting |

## Attuned Mind (2nd Level)

**Benefit:** Develops strong sense of arcana and resistance to attacks on mental state.

### Specific Advantages:
- Advantage when resisting mind-related attacks (charm, fear, madness, etc.)
- Advantage when sensing magic

## Battle Magic (7th Level)

**Uses:** Can reroll any or all spell damage dice, OR reroll number of targets affected by spell
- Each die to reroll costs one Reroll Pool die

---

## Tower (10th Level)

At 10th level, build tower for yourself to house:
- Library
- Laboratory
- etc.

### Attracts:
- Mixed bag of mercenaries
- Strange servants
- An apprentice
- Perhaps even a few monsters

**Relationship:** Motley crew follows orders for as long as they dare

## Designer's Notes

> ⚠️ **Important Context:** In original LFG, Magic User class encompassed ALL forms of magic. Cultist class changes this by creating implicit divide between arcane and divine power (even though combined spell list remains).
- GMs should ignore/modify rules they don't like, especially classes
- Some Unique Features provide further options: Divine Blessings, Spell Casting, Faustian Pact
