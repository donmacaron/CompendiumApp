# Fighter Class Guide

## Overview

> "You are a fighter, a veteran warrior expertly trained in weapons and armour. Perhaps you are a mail-clad knight, an agile spearman, or a sharp-eyed bowman."

**Core Philosophy:**
- Most heavily armed and armoured adventurer in the game
- Comfortable in thick of battle
- Rely on brawn, steel and superior skill to win day
- Versatile masters of all weapons, switching between armaments and fighting styles

### Key Attributes
- **Primary:** Strength and/or Dexterity (choice)

### Hit Points
| Level Range | HP Formula | HP Bonus |
|-------------|------------|----------|
| Up to 9th | 1d5 + 5 (+Con if any) per level | — |
| 10th+ | 3 hp/level | — |

### Equipment
- **Armour:** Any (versatile class)
- **Shields:** Allowed
- **Weapons:** Any (most versatile in weapons)

### Skills (Choose at character creation)
- **Proficient:** Leadership, Athletics
- **Additional (+4):** Acrobatics, Arcane Lore, Deception, Detection, Divine Lore, Apothecary, Gather Information, General Lore, Insight, Persuasion, Sailing, Stealth, Traps & Locks, Wilderness Lore

## Adaptable (1st Level)

### Uses: 1 per level per adventure
**Regain:** Short/long rest (p.81)

### Benefits of Default Style
- Gain benefits of default style at all times (except as noted below)

### Switching Styles
At level gain: Can substitute default style for another if desired
**Mechanic:** When using Adaptable, choose one known style and substitute for default until end of combat
**Usage:** Can use Adaptable multiple times during same combat
**Reverting:** Can revert to original default style at any time (no action, no Adaptable use)

### Equipment Advantage
Using Adaptable is part of normal action; sheathing/stowing/readying armaments including shield costs **no action cost**

## Fighter Style Examples

| Style | Description & Benefits |
|-------|------------------------|
| **Charger** | When charging: enemies don't gain advantage on attacks against you. If hit, target must make Luck (Str) save or be knocked prone/pushed up to 10 ft |
| **Dual Weapons** | Armed with two one-handed melee weapons: On every miss with extra attack, gain +1 to hit on subsequent extra attacks until it hits (cumulative) |
| **Long Reach** | With 10 ft reach weapon: Block 10 ft diameter area instead of 5 ft. On Nat 19, may trip foe |
| **Opportunist** | Once per turn, when reducing foe to zero HP with weapon attack, immediately make one free melee/thrown attack (declare after reducing target) |
| **Protector** | Automatically succeed on Dex checks for Rescue Exploits; shields grant +2 AC instead of +1 |
| **Ranged** | Targets lose up to 2 points of AC bonus from cover. Never reroll miss against ally in same melee |
| **Single Weapon** | With one-handed weapon and empty other hand: +2 bonus to initiative, interceptions, Luck (Dex) saves, reaction-based Dex checks |
| **Two Hander** | Melee two-handed weapons: Advantage when rolling damage |
| **Rearguard** | Declare at start of round before your turn: Adopt defensive manoeuvres, direct tactical retreat guiding allies to safety. All party members gain advantage on Party Retreat tests |
| **Unarmed** | Unarmed attacks cause 1d6 damage instead of 1d2. On Nat 19, grab/disarm foe |

## Deadly Strikes (1st Level)

### Critical Hit Threshold
- Weapon attacks cause critical hits on natural 1d20 roll of **19-20**

### Special Effects Enhancement
If weapon causes special effect on natural 19, effect also occurs on natural 20

---

## Supplies (2nd Level)

**Activation:** At any time during adventure
**Benefit:** Automatically add 1d4 mundane items to equipment list
- Items must have common theme (example: coils of rope, grappling hook, climbing boots; cache of fire pots, etc.)
- Particularly expensive/bulky items may not be permitted (GM discretion)
**Cooldown:** After using, cannot use again until resupply at village/town/city

## Master of Arms (5th Level)

### Critical Hit Threshold
- Weapon attacks cause critical hits on natural 1d20 roll of **18-20** (improved from 19-20)

### Special Effects Enhancement
If weapon causes special effect on natural 19, effect also occurs on:
- Natural 18 or 20

### Counter Measure
When hit by melee/ranged attack:
- Spend Reroll die to force opponent to reroll their attack and use lowest result

---

## Second Attack (7th Level)

Spend a Reroll die to make second attack on your turn.
- Note: If two-weapon fighting, you don't gain second extra attack with this ability

---

## Stronghold (10th Level)

Establish stronghold and attract body of loyal men-at-arms who swear fealty.

### Development Potential
- Keep attracts villagers over time
- Earn title or rights to collect taxes
