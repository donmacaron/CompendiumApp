# Barbarian Class Guide

## Overview

> "You are a barbarian, a savage warrior from the tundra, mountains, deep forests or sandy wastes. You might be a Viking raider, a Conan like behemoth or an Amazonian warrior woman."

**Key Traits:**
- Long exposure to harsh elements has equipped you with primal ferocity, acute instincts, formidable endurance
- Well versed in weapons
- Prefer armours that do not hinder natural athleticism
- Usually superstitious, illiterate, distrust magic

### Key Attributes
- **Primary:** Constitution

### Hit Points
| Level Range | HP Formula | HP Bonus |
|-------------|------------|----------|
| Up to 9th | 1d6 + 6 (+Con if any) per level | — |
| 10th+ | 4 hp/level | — |

### Equipment
- **Armour:** Light, Medium (no heavy)
- **Shields:** Allowed
- **Weapons:** Any except crossbows

### Skills (Choose at character creation)
- **Proficient:** Animal Lore, Wilderness Lore
- **Additional (+4):** Acrobatics, Athletics, Apothecary, Deception, Detection, Divine Lore, Gather Information, Insight, Leadership, Persuasion, Sailing, Stealth

---

## Ferocious Rage (1st & 7th Level)

**Uses:** 1 per Int modifier per adventure

### Activating Rage
- Declare during your turn OR
- Trigger in response to damage OR charm/fear/madness effect during another's turn
- Regain uses via short/long rest

### Rage Duration
- **Rounds:** Equal to Con modifier
- Note: If triggered after another's turn, that round doesn't count

### Rage Effects
| Effect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Bonus on Str/Con checks | +2 |
| Bonus on attack rolls | +2 |
| Bonus on damage rolls | +2 |
| Bonus on Luck saves | +2 |
| Immunity to/suspend charm, fear, madness effects | — |
| Damage reduction | All damage halved |
| Noise level | Loud/guttural (ruins stealth) |

### Restrictions While Raging
- Can speak, but most words incomprehensible
- Cannot cast spells

### At 7th Level: Death Saving Fumble
If raging and reduced to zero HP, make a Luck (Con) save to be reduced to 1 HP instead.

---

## Born to the Wilds (1st Level)

**Benefit:** Advantage on Wilderness Lore checks related to survival skills

---

## Sixth Sense (2nd Level)

**Benefit:** Advantage when making checks to resist ambush or surprise

---

## Killer Instinct (5th Level)

### Critical Hits
- Critical hit threshold improved to 1d20 roll of **19-20** (was natural 20)

### Finishing Move
Weapon attacks that reduce target to **single digit HP (9 or less)** deal lethal damage instead.

---

## Second Attack (7th Level)

Spend a Reroll die to make a second attack on your turn.
- Note: If two-weapon fighting, you don't gain a second extra attack with this ability

---

## Chieftain (10th Level)

Attract or take control of a tribe of barbarians who swear fealty to you.

### As Tribal Head
- Direct the tribe's activities
- Tribe develops unmatched knowledge of relevant area
- Some mark out territories, others follow shifting migration patterns
