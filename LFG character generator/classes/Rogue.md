# Rogue Class Guide

## Overview

> "You are a rogue, a lurker in the shadows, gifted in acrobatics, stealth and sleight of hand. You might be a brave scout, a sly thief, a deadly assassin or a haughty pirate."

**Core Philosophy:**
- Agility and cunning are hallmarks
- Lightly armoured
- Relies on speed and guile to keep from harm
- Employs range of tricks and deceits to maintain upper hand
- When strike, sword comes without warning, dropping opponents before they realise true threat

### Key Attributes
- **Primary:** Dexterity

### Hit Points
| Level Range | HP Formula | HP Bonus |
|-------------|------------|----------|
| Up to 9th | 1d4 + 4 (+Con if any) per level | — |
| 10th+ | 2 hp/level | — |

### Equipment
- **Armour:** Light only (no heavy armour, no shields mentioned)
- **Shields:** Not allowed
- **Weapons:** One-handed weapons, light crossbow, short bow and sling

### Skills (Choose at character creation)
- **Proficient:** Detection, Sleight of Hand, Stealth, Traps & Locks
- **Additional (+4):** Acrobatics, Arcane Lore, Athletics, Apothecary, Deception, Divine Lore, Insight, Gather Information, General Lore, Persuasion, Sailing

## Backstab (1st and 5th Level)

### Mechanics
**Frequency:** Once per turn

When making melee attack against target that is surprised by you:
- Gain **+4 to hit** AND inflict additional damage

### Damage Progression
| Level | Additional Damage |
|-------|------------------|
| 1st level | 1d8 |
| 5th level (upgrades) | 2d8 |

---

## Finisher (1st Level)

### Trigger
Staggered target (p.79), after successfully hit with melee attack

### Effect
May add backstab damage

**Note:** Once per adventurer (uses limited, same as other abilities)

## Tricks & Techniques (1st Level)

### Learning System
At 1st level: Know number of tricks = Dex modifier
- Each level thereafter: Learn one additional trick
- Can substitute known trick for another
- **Uses:** 1 per level per adventure
- **Regain:** Short/long rest (p.81)
- Apply as part of normal action

### Trick List

| Trick | Effect |
|-------|--------|
| **Cat's Grace** | Subject to GM discretion: May turn failed save/check relating to climbing, acrobatics or falling into success |
| **Choking Dust** | Up to 1d4 targets within 5 ft must make Luck (Con) save or be rendered mute for 1d4 rounds |
| **Blind Sense** | By applying proper techniques: Temporarily heightens senses, halving usual penalties for poor visibility. Effect lasts 1d6 × 10 minutes |
| **Flash Powder** | Up to 1d4 targets within 5 ft must make Luck (Dex) save or be blinded until end of their next turn |
| **Glue Pot** | On failed Luck (Str) save: One target within 30 ft cannot move from current position until they use action to break free with successful Str check. Targets with Str 19+ are immune |
| **Hidden Blade** | May reroll a failed melee attack |
| **Quick Reflexes** | May reroll initiative check and choose best result |
| **Rapid Dose** | May apply single dose of poison, OR drink potion (or similar) |
| **Slippery Mind** | When subject to magical forces detecting thoughts or locating her: May make Int check to fool/mislead/negate effect. Lasts 1d6 × 10 minutes |
| **Smoke Bomb** | Throw up to 40 ft. Thick smoke fills 15 ft diameter, blinding those within AND blocking line of sight. Smoke disperses in 1d4 rounds |
| **Unseen Whip** | On failed Luck (Dex) save: One target within 10 ft is tripped, pulled up to 10 ft, OR disarmed |

### Tool Expertise Note
> Generally speaking, other classes do not have expertise required to make effective use of these specialised tools and techniques.

## Skirmisher (2nd Level and 7th Level)

### At 2nd Level: Natural Skirmisher
**Benefit:** Attacking swiftly then disengaging before target can properly retaliate

When subject to free attack due to movement:
- Enemy suffers disadvantage on attack roll

### At 7th Level: Enhanced Defence
**Action:** Spend Reroll die
**Effect:** Turn the hit into a miss (if hit by such attack)

## Lethal Precision (7th Level)

### Reroll System
Reroll any or all of backstab damage dice AND use best result

**Cost:** Each damage die rerolled costs one Reroll die

---

## Thieves' Guild (10th Level)

Establish guild formed of local thieves, cutpurses and scoundrels.

### Guild Characteristics
- Shadowy figures surprisingly loyal
- Willing to take great risks for their Guildmaster
- Condition: As long as opportunities flow for them

**Role:** Guildmaster keeps opportunities flowing; guild operates as shadow network at character's direction

## Design Notes

- LFG system classes
- Rogue represents stealth expert, acrobat, thief and assassin
- Lightly armoured but highly skilled in sneak attacks and tricks
- Unique skill set with limited equipment access (other classes can't use rogues' specialized tools effectively)
