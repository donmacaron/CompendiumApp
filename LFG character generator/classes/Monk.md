# Monk Class Guide

## Overview

> "You are a monk, a pilgrim adventurer and martial artist, travelling the wilds in search of enlightenment. You might be a peacekeeping vagabond, a reclusive mystic or a vigilante drifter."

**Core Philosophy:**
- Raised by order, lack family attachment, eschew material things
- Focus on perfect alignment of mind, body and spirit
- Unarmed and unarmoured
- Lifetime of training forged into fearsome weapon
- "Strong as mountain and supple as wind"
- Embodiment of discipline; warrior spirit burns brighter than sun

### Key Attributes
- **Primary:** Willpower

### Hit Points
| Level Range | HP Formula | HP Bonus |
|-------------|------------|----------|
| Up to 9th | 1d4 + 4 (+Con if any) per level | — |
| 10th+ | 2 hp/level | — |

### Equipment
- **Armour:** None (unarmoured class)
- **Shields:** Not allowed
- **Weapons:** Spear, shortsword, dagger, staff and sling (limited but not restricted weapons)

### Skills (Choose at character creation)
- **Proficient:** Acrobatics, Apothecary, Athletics
- **Additional (+4):** Arcane Lore, Deception, Detection, Divine Lore, General Lore, Insight, Persuasion, Sailing, Stealth, Traps & Locks, Wilderness Lore

## Martial Arts (1st Level)

### Unarmed Combat
Monks are masters of unarmed combat:
- Damage: **1d6** (plus Strength modifier) with hands, feet and other body parts

### Extra Attacks
May make extra attack each turn as if armed with two weapons:
- Punch, kick, headbutt, etc.
- This extra attack does NOT suffer disadvantage
- Exception: If monk also using weapon two-handed (p.76)

---

## Monk Techniques (1st Level)

### Learning System
At 1st level: Know number of techniques = Will modifier
- Each level thereafter: Learn one additional technique
- Can substitute known technique for another
- **Uses:** 1 per level per adventure
- **Regain:** Short/long rest (p.81)
- Apply as part of normal action

### Technique List

| d20 | Technique (Flavor Name) | Effect |
|-----|-------------------------|--------|
| 1 | **Acrobatics** (Formless Water) | Until start of next turn: Bonus = twice Will modifier to AC, Luck (Dex) saves, defensive Dex checks. May be triggered after being hit/targeted (potentially turning hit into miss, etc.) |
| 2 | **Channel Spirit** (Unchain the Dragon) | Channels spirit into unarmed strikes: Critical hits on natural 19-20 attack rolls; bypasses immunity/suppresses regeneration vs non-magical weapons. Duration = Will modifier rounds |
| 3 | **Deflect Projectile** (Moon shields Sun) | When damaged by non-magical ranged attack (dagger, arrow, boulder, etc.): Can be triggered to negate it |
| 4 | **Strength of One** | Increases Str to 19 for one action |
| 5 | **Impossible Leap** (Heaven's Leap) | May jump up to 30 ft in any direction. Some GMs might require staff/creature/terrain to launch from |
| 6 | **Mind over Matter** | Can delay effect of bodily injury until next long rest. Only one injury may be delayed at a time |
| 7 | **Moment of Clarity** | Until start of next turn: Ignores penalties due to poor visibility; pinpoints invisible or hidden foes within 30 ft |
| 8 | **Perfection of Will** | When subject to effect requiring Luck (Will) save, can make Will check instead |
| 9 | **Purity of Self** | When subject to disease/poison effect (magical or otherwise): Make Will check to negate it |
| 10 | **Redirect Attack** (Fork the River) | When damaged by melee attack: Make Will check to transfer damage to another enemy instead (within reach of monk or original attacker) |
| 11 | **Stunning Strike** (Iron Fist) | On failed Luck (Will) save, one target within reach loses its next action. Gigantic creatures may be immune (GM discretion) |

## Open Hand Versatility (2nd Level)

### Subduing Opponents
Monks are experts in controlling and subduing their opponents.

**Effect:** On natural 19 unarmed attack roll:
- May grab, trip, disarm OR push target up to 10 ft (choice)

## Enlightened Warrior (5th Level)

### Benefits Gained:

#### Open Hand Versatility Enhancement
- Now applies on natural 19-20 attack roll (expanded from just 19)

#### Insight Ability
- Once per adventure, after period of meditation/contemplation
- Spend Reroll die to gain useful insight
- Examples GM might provide:
  - Important piece of information about party goals
  - Spotlight overlooked clue or connection
  - Provide other inspiration

#### End Magic Effect
- Once per adventure, during your turn
- Spend Reroll die to end magical effect you are subject to
- Note: If others are also subject to same effect, does not end for them

---

## Counter Attack (7th Level)

**Trigger:** After being damaged by enemy
**Action:** Spend Reroll die to make single unarmed attack against that foe
**Restriction:** May not be used more than once per round

---

## Monastery (10th Level)

Establish monastery complete with:
- Dedicated novices
- Handful of older monks to guide them

As Grandmaster: Set order's philosophy and objectives (need not align with original order)
