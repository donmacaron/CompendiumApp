# Bard Class Guide

## Overview

> "You are a bard, a charismatic wanderer; part artist, diplomat and warrior, as skilled with blades as you are with ballads. In your travels you draw knowledge from all corners, forging alliances and extracting secrets from those who should know better."

**Core Philosophy:**
- A bard's influence fortifies the company
- Strengthening bonds and common will to prevail
- With right words and deeds, elevates party beyond sum of parts

### Key Attributes
- **Primary:** Charisma

### Hit Points
| Level Range | HP Formula | HP Bonus |
|-------------|------------|----------|
| Up to 9th | 1d4 + 4 (+Con if any) per level | — |
| 10th+ | 2 hp/level | — |

### Equipment
- **Armour:** Light, Medium
- **Shields:** Allowed
- **Weapons:** Any (versatile class)

### Skills (Choose any 7 at character creation)
- Animal Lore, Apothecary, Arcane Lore, Athletics, Deception, Detection, Divine Lore, Gather Information, General Lore, Insight, Leadership, Persuasion, Sailing, Sleight of Hand, Stealth, Traps & Locks, Wilderness Lore

---

## Inspire Greatness (1st Level)

**Uses:** 1 per level per adventure
**Regain:** Short/long rest

### Activation (no action cost)
- Provided ally within **120 ft** and can see/hear you
- Grant ally bonus equal to Cha modifier on one roll
- Can use ability AFTER seeing roll result
- Can use on your own rolls if you can see/hear an ally within 120 ft

---

## Silver Tongued (1st Level)

**Benefits:**
- Advantage when taking actions related to:
  - Persuasion skill (choose one of these two)
  - Deception skill
- Advantage on Gather Information checks
- Advantage conducting performances to entertain/inspire

---

## Bardic Knowledge (2nd Level)

### Language Skills
- Make a Cha check to understand/communicate in language you don't formally know
- Speaking: Short sentences only
- Comprehension: Gist of what is said/written

### Lore Advantage
Have advantage when recalling lore relating to:
- History
- Culture (including myths and legends)
- Etiquette

## Rare Fighting Techniques (5th Level)

### Critical Hits
- Weapon attacks cause critical hits on natural **1d20 roll of 19-20**

### Ally Support
Provided ally within 120 ft and can see/hear you:
- They may reroll any fumbled attack rolls
- No Reroll die cost
- Can use ability to reroll your own fumbled weapon attacks (if seeing/hearing ally)

---

## Rallying Shout (7th Level)

**Uses:** Once per round, spend a Reroll die
**Range:** Ally within 120 ft who can see/hear you
**Effect:** Reroll failed weapon attack
- Can also reroll your own failed weapon attacks (if seeing/hearing ally)

---

## Network of Informants (10th Level)

At 10th level, establish a network of contacts with yourself at centre.

### Network Characteristics
- Spans multiple cities
- Covers broad spectrum of society
- Members are eclectic bunch with skills in most fields

As principal:
- Set network's overall objectives
- Direct activities (as much as possible)
