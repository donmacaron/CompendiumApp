# Artificer Class Guide

## Overview

> "Hammer in hand and hellfire at your belt, you travel abroad in search of inspiration, putting your unique devices to the test. Most folk brand you a menace; a heretic meddling in secret and unstable forces, destined for an early grave. And perhaps they're right."

### Key Attributes
- **Key Attribute:** Intelligence (Min 15)

### Hit Points
| Level | HP Formula | HP Bonus |
|-------|------------|----------|
| Up to 9th | 1d3 + 3 (+Con if any) per level | — |
| 10th+ | 1 hp/level | — |

### Equipment
- **Armour:** Light, Medium
- **Shields:** Allowed
- **Weapons:** One-handed weapons, light crossbow, sling

### Skills (Choose at character creation)
- **Proficient:** Apothecary, General Lore
- **Additional (+4):** Acrobatics, Animal Lore, Athletics, Deception, Detection, Gather Information, Insight, Persuasion, Sailing, Sleight of Hand, Stealth, Traps & Locks

## Alchemy & Mechanica (1st Level)

At 1st level, you can craft inventions equal to your Int modifier. Each level thereafter, you create one additional invention. Uses are regained on rest.

### Prototype Inventions

#### Black Powder Weapon
- **Type:** Two-handed, Range: 120 ft
- **Damage:** 4d4 (reroll 4s), accumulates on critical 19
- **Effects:** Loud, prevents stealth
- **On fumble:** Malfunctions to club until repaired

#### Breathing Mask
- Activates air supply or counters gas attacks
- Immune to gas attacks
- Underwater: 1d6 × 10 mins
- Optional: Can share with allies (GM discretion)

#### Chaintooth Weapon
- **Type:** Two-handed mechanized melee (chainsaw, piston hammer, etc.)
- **Damage:** 2d4+1 (reroll 4s)
- **Critical 19:** Random body injury roll 1d6 + Injuries & Setbacks table

#### Corroding Spray
- Range: 5 ft to metallic object (weapon/armor)
- Effect: Rust, crack, or destroy
- **Save:** Luck (Dex) if carried
- **Immune:** Enchanted objects, special metals (e.g., silvered)

#### Flash Rig
- When hit by sight-based opponent attack, spend action to activate blinding flash
- **Effect:** 66% miss chance on the attack

#### Fume Flask
- Range: 90 ft
- Area: 10 ft radius, duration: 1d6 × 10 mins
- **Effects (choose one):**
  - Insidious Slumber
  - Fetid Fog of the Rotting Horde
  - Writhing Fog

#### Hellfire Glass
- Range: 90 ft
- Area: 10 ft radius explosion, 2d4 + 1d4 per odd level beyond 1st (no save)
- **Reroll:** 4s reroll and accumulate
- **Restriction:** Cannot use within enemy's melee reach
- **Trap capable:** Fuse, timer, tripwire allowed

#### Ironward
- When hit by metal-based attack (sword, arrow, trap blade)
- **Action:** Int check to activate magnetic pulse
- **Effect:** Deflects attack at last moment

#### Thunder Gauntlet
- Range: 5 ft melee touch
- **Damage:** 2d4 × 5 ft knockback
- **Save:** Luck (Con) to resist
- Note: Large targets/fixed objects generally immune (GM discretion)
- Can launch yourself using this ability

#### Mutagen (Various Concoctions)
- Active duration: 2d6 rounds, only one at a time
- **50% risk:** 1 point of attribute loss on expiration (excluding Int)
- Roll 1d8 for effect:

| d8 Result | Effect |
|-----------|--------|
| 1 | Strength increases to 17 |
| 2 | Dexterity increases to 17 |
| 3 | Strength & Dex increase to 17 |
| 4 | Spit poison at single target up to 15 ft (Luck/Con save or paralysed 1d4 hours) |
| 5 | Eye-watering fumes in 5 ft (all creatures make Luck/Con save or blinded until next turn) |
| 6 | As Hunger for Blood (self only) |
| 7 | As Demonic Convergence |
| 8 | Restore 1d10 HP each round on your turn (ends at 0 HP) |

#### Truth Serum
- Method: Injected or ingested
- **Effect:** Target blurt out honest answers for 2d6 rounds
- **Save:** Luck (Will) to resist

#### Xray Goggles
- Duration: 1 round
- Vision: 30 ft cone with 10 ft base
- Penetrates: Metal, stone, earth, wood (1 ft each)
- Blocked by: Thin sheet of lead

## Troubleshooter (2nd Level)

**Frequency:** Once per Int modifier per adventure

You can improvise jury-rigged devices or ad-hoc mixtures to bypass obstacles.

**Requirements:** Successful Int check (Reroll Pool available), modified at GM's discretion.

### Example Improvised Devices
- Primitive but serviceable parachute
- Tailored acid melting locks before inert
- One-shot flare gun or magnetic grapple
- Fusing agent to bind inanimate objects
- Sticky gloves for climbing sheer surfaces

**Duration:** Up to 1d4 hours (temporary, makeshift)
**Cost:** Complex items may cost 1 point of Luck (GM option)

## Major Breakthrough (5th Level)

### Benefits
| Benefit | Details |
|---------|---------|
| New Alchemy & Mechanica | Create new invention during downtime, add to known abilities |
| Field Repair | Once per adventure: spend 1d6 × 10 mins to repair broken/damaged item in field |
| Replacement Limb | During downtime (cost ≥1,000 gp): permanently replace limb with clockwork/steam/canned lightning |

### Replacement Limb Effects
- Subject loses 1 Con but gains +1 AC
- Possible features: Secret compartments, tool/weapon attachments (GM option)
- Max one limb per subject

## Supercharged (7th Level)

Reroll damage dice from Alchemy & Mechanica abilities or initial Mutagen roll.
**Cost:** Each die rerolled costs one Reroll die.

---

## House of Gears (10th Level)

Establish an artificing facility complete with:
- Mine, forge, workshop, laboratory, apprentices

As **Master Mechanica**: Set facility goals/objectives and direct crews' activities.
