# Ranger Class Guide

## Overview

> "You are a ranger, a hunter of monsters and men; scout of the borderlands and sentinel of civilization. You might be a faultless tracker, a brave woodsmen, a grizzled beastmaster or a veteran explorer."

**Core Philosophy:**
- Bold and self-reliant
- Skill with bow rightly feared
- Herbalist lore respected
- Many consider lone wolf
- Party allies and beast friend provide fellowship needed
- Beholden to none; alone decide fate
- Free as bird and boundless as sky

### Key Attributes
- **Primary:** Perception

### Hit Points
| Level Range | HP Formula | HP Bonus |
|-------------|------------|----------|
| Up to 9th | 1d4 + 4 (+Con if any) per level | — |
| 10th+ | 2 hp/level | — |

### Equipment
- **Armour:** Light, Medium only (no heavy armour)
- **Shields:** Allowed
- **Weapons:** One-handed weapons; any ranged weapons EXCEPT heavy crossbow

### Skills (Choose at character creation)
- **Proficient:** Animal Lore, Wilderness Lore, Stealth
- **Additional (+3):** Acrobatics, Athletics, Apothecary, Arcane Lore, Deception, Detection, Divine Lore, General Lore, Insight, Persuasion, Sailing, Traps & Locks

## Beast Companion (1st Level)

### Acquisition
**Timing:** During downtime between adventures
**Selection:** Can gain beast companion

### Companion Characteristics
- Especially smart, brave and loyal animal
- Small to moderate size
- Three examples provided: Wolf, Viper, Owl

### Special Bond
Two share special bond; communicate using sounds, touch, hand gestures, body language, etc.

## Beast Companion Statistics & Benefits

| Statistic | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Hit Points** | Maximum (plus 2 hp per ranger level) |
| **To Hit Bonus** | Based on ranger's level (per Advancement Table) |
| **Critical Hit Damage** | Based on ranger's level (per Advancement Table) |
| **Skills** | One skill reasonably applicable to animal (Stealth, Athletics, etc.) |
| **Luck Attribute** | Equal to ranger's current Luck. Ranger's Luck is NOT reduced on successful save by companion |
| **PC Treatment** | Treated as PC for: morale, death, injuries, healing |
| **Reroll Pool** | Shares the ranger's Reroll Pool |

## Rangercraft (1st Level)

### Learning System
At 1st level: Know number of talents = Perc modifier
- Each level thereafter: Learn one additional talent
- Can substitute known talent for another
- **Uses:** 1 per level per adventure
- **Regain:** Short/long rest (p.81)
- Apply as part of normal action

### Talent List

| Number | Talent Name | Effect |
|--------|-------------|--------|
| 1 | **Animal Ken** | Ranger and beast companion may briefly exchange detailed information, including matters which might not normally be imparted by animal (GM discretion). GM might require Perc check for especially complex information |
| 2 | **Anti-toxin Draught** | One target within reach applies Anti-toxin effects. Additionally: 1d4 attribute points lost due to poison restored over 1d6 hours |
| 3 | **Instinctive Rescue** | Beast companion may perform Rescue targeting ranger. If GM agrees Rescue is possible: Companion makes Dex check and ranger makes Perc check. If successful, Rescue occurs. No Luck check required. Not available if companion incapacitated |
| 4 | **Nature's Venom** | After hitting with weapon attack: May impose 75% chance target's next turn occurs last in initiative order (poison effect) |
| 5 | **Off Hand Adept** | Two-weapon fighting: Before rolling to hit with extra attack this turn, may choose to negate usual disadvantage penalty for that attack roll |
| 6 | **Cover Fire** | If ranger or ally hit by ranged attack: May force attacker to reroll that attack and use lowest result. Ranger must be armed with ranged/thrown weapon AND within range of attacker to activate |
| 7 | **Ranger's Ointment** | For 1d6+6 hours: One target within reach gains advantage on rolls to resist adverse effects from cold/heat/fire/lightning (magical or otherwise) |
| 8 | **Seasoned Explorer** | Gains advantage on all checks related to Wilderness Lore. Once known, always active (does not require Rangercraft use) |
| 9 | **Sharpshooter** | Before rolling damage with ranged attack: Make Perc check. If successful, attack causes critical hit damage. May not be used more than once per round |
| 10 | **Veteran Scout** | For 1d6 × 10 minutes: Ranger and beast companion leave no tracks; ignore armour penalties; move stealthily without slowing normal move rate (if GM normally requires such) |
| 11 | **Slip Away** | Gains Rogue's Skirmisher ability until end of her next turn |

## Steady Shot (2nd Level)

### Ranged Attack Benefits:
- Targets lose up to 2 points of AC bonus due to cover
- If miss target: Never reroll attack against ally in same melee (LFG p.73)

## Master Hunter (5th Level)

### Ranged Attack Benefits
- Ranged attacks cause critical hits on natural 1d20 roll of **19-20**

### Beast Companion Upgrades
| Improvement | Detail |
|-------------|--------|
| Hit Points | Increase by 3 hp per ranger level (instead of 2) |
| Skirmisher Ability | Gains Rogue Skirmisher ability using ranger level. If already has, gains +1 AC instead |

## Second Attack (7th Level)

Spend Reroll die to make second attack on turn.
- Note: If two-weapon fighting, don't gain second extra attack with this ability

---

## Outpost (10th Level)

Secure outpost as base of operations in wilds.

### Outpost Characteristics
- Patrolled and maintained by rotating band of:
  - Independent trackers
  - Hunters
  - Allied beasts

### As High Ranger
Guide band's activities AND develop intimate knowledge of area including:
- Enemy settlements
- Threats
- Secret trails
- etc.
