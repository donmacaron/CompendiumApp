# Cultist Class Guide

## Overview

> "You are a cultist, the devoted servant of a god, daemon or other cosmic entity distant and inscrutable. You might be a pious priestess, an unflinching inquisitor, or a haunted warlock struggling to avert a terrible doom."

**Core Philosophy:**
- Like all travellers of perilous lands, familiar with weapons and armour
- Mindful that the divine helps those who help themselves
- In your hour of need, when flesh fails and shield splinters, call upon goddess for aid

### Key Attributes
- **Primary:** Willpower

### Hit Points
| Level Range | HP Formula | HP Bonus |
|-------------|------------|----------|
| Up to 9th | 1d4 + 4 (+Con if any) per level | — |
| 10th+ | 2 hp/level | — |

### Equipment
- **Armour:** Light, Medium (and shields)
- **Weapons:** Bludgeoning weapons and any sacred to cult's ethos (GM determines)

### Skills (Choose at character creation)
- **Proficient:** Apothecary, Divine Lore
- **Additional (+4):** Arcane Lore, Athletics, Deception, Detection, Gather Information, General Lore, Insight, Persuasion, Sailing, Stealth, Wilderness Lore

## Blessings System (1st Level)

As agent of gods, cultists may petition patron for supernatural blessings and miracles.

### Blessing Pool
At 1st level: Know number of blessings = Will modifier
- Each level thereafter: Learn one additional blessing
- Can substitute known blessings for others
- **Uses:** 1 per level per adventure
- **Regain:** Short/long rest (p.81)

When using Blessings: Choose one known blessing to apply each time

---

## Available Blessings

All cultists can learn these blessings:

### Blessed Weapon
- One weapon touched counts as magical for 1d6 × 10 minutes
- Activation: No action cost

### Holy Smite
- As part of melee attack, instead of rolling damage:
  - Deal maximum weapon damage + your level
- Attack counts as magical
- **Restriction:** May not be used more than once per round

### Lay on Hands
- Spend action to heal touched target
- **Healing:** 1d4 HP per cultist level + Will modifier
- If target at zero HP: Wounds mend slowly over 1d3 minutes
  - Target does NOT regain consciousness until 1d3 minutes passes

### Mend Injury (Argona Variant)
- Same as Solace of Argona
- **Restriction:** Cannot be used more than half cultist's level (round up) per adventure

### Purge Affliction (Baal Variant)
- Same as Purge the Accursed
- **Restriction:** Cannot be used more than half cultist's level (round up) per adventure

### Sense Abomination
- Spend action to detect Undead, Demons and Aberrations within 120 ft
- Can determine their direction
- Duration: 2d6 rounds

### Spell-like Blessing
Each time chosen, gain blessing mimicking one of following spells:
- Pierce the Veil
- Lucent Emanation
- Sever Arcanum
- Cleansing Charm

### Turning
- Spend action to brandish holy symbol
- Target: 1d8 + level Undead/Demons/Aberrations within 60 ft
- **HD Threshold Rules:**
  - Less than half cultist's HD: Automatically destroyed (no save)
  - Equal to or lower: Gain Luck save to resist fleeing for 1d6 × 10 mins
  - More HD than cultist: Unaffected

### Watched Over
- Target: Cultist and all PC allies within 20 ft
- **Effect:** +1 AC as long as cultist is above zero HP
- Note: Once known, always active (does not expend blessing use)

## Tenets & Favour System

### How Favour is Gained
- Must take action promoting/enacting/adhering to one or more tenets
- GM determines whether tenet is met and Favour bestowed
- Cultist either HAS favour OR does not have it
- Acting contrary strips favour (GM discretion, or Luck/Cha save might be called)

### Consequences of Violating Tenets
- Repeated violations may impose penalties on invoking blessings
- May prevent any blessings until atonement complete (GM's call)

### Divine Rebuke
- If labouring under rebuke requiring Atonement:
  - Cannot gain Favour until atonement complete

### GM Recommendations
- Give PCs benefit of doubt when deciding if tenet satisfied
- Repeatedly using same tenet to garner favour may become increasingly difficult

### Example Scenario (Priestess of Argona)
| Time | Action | Result |
|------|--------|--------|
| Dawn | Bolster allies' spirits with inspiring hymn (Foster hope) | Favour gained |
| Hour later | Heal wounds mid-combat (Aid the sick and injured) | Favour regained |
| Dusk | Try to foster hope again | GM requires one-hour ceremony instead of short prayer |

## Example Gods & Tenets

### 1. Argona (The Starmaiden)
- **Domains:** Health, wealth, happiness, hope; fierce protector of families (especially children, elderly, vulnerable)
- **Depicted:** Beautiful raven-haired woman with trail of stars in lieu of legs
- **Related Activities:** Weddings, births, carousing, trade deals, healing, defending kith and kin
- **Feast Day:** Thanksgiving

**Common Phrases:**
- "Argona protects"
- "Starmaiden keep you"
- "Health, wealth and happiness"

**Common Icons:** Star/stars; Shield with star motif

#### Tenets
| Number | Tenet |
|--------|-------|
| 1 | Foster hope |
| 2 | Aid the sick and injured |
| 3 | Fight for those who cannot fight for themselves |
| 4 | Embrace toil and prosper |
| 5 | Celebrate good works |

#### Patron Benefits
1. Advantage on Apothecary-related checks
2. Additional Spell-like Blessings:
   - Deny the Nine Furies
   - None Shall Pass
   - Ward of Lost Souls

---

### 2. Baal (Lord of Crows)
- **Domains:** Decay, suffering, disease and death
- **Common avoidance:** Name commonly uttered to ward off unwanted attention
- **Depicted:** Floating skull, murder of crows, or animate ooze
- **Related Activities:** Funerals, executions, palliative care, ancestor worship, spreading/resisting disease, torture, managing/ending suffering
- **Feast Day:** Day of Dust

**Common Phrases:**
- "No life without death"
- "Bones and dust, blood and rust. All are equal before Baal"
- "Baal's Balls!"

**Common Icons:** Skull chalice; Crow(s); Ooze

#### Tenets
| Number | Tenet |
|--------|-------|
| 1 | Pray for the departed |
| 2 | Heed the suffering of others |
| 3 | End those that deserve death |
| 4 | Purge undead and demons wherever they roam |
| 5 | Stifle disease |
| 6 | It is blessed to repurpose that which is in decay |

#### Patron Benefits
1. **Shadow of Death:** On natural 20 melee attack roll, living target with less HD than cultist dies (no save)
   - Magical effect requiring DDM check
   - Can choose not to invoke this effect
2. Additional Spell-like Blessings: Riddle of Bones

---

### 3. Graxus (The Iron God)
- **Domains:** War, courage, struggle and glory
- **Depicted:** Juggernaut of destruction; towering half-man, half-iron fusing of steel and flesh
- **Related Activities:** Warfare, combat, fisticuffs, arm wrestling, exhortations of grit, bestowing accolades
- **Feast Day:** Ironvow

**Common Phrases:**
- "By blood or blade!"
- "Victory and death!"
- "The Iron God cometh!"

**Common Icons:** Anvil and skull; Iron fist; Crossed swords with central eye

#### Tenets
| Number | Tenet |
|--------|-------|
| 1 | Test your mettle |
| 2 | Live boldly or not at all |
| 3 | Finish your foe without hesitation |
| 4 | Iron and steel are hallowed metals; treat them so |
| 5 | Bring glory to yourself and the Iron God |

#### Patron Benefits
1. **Trained for War:** Can use ALL melee weapons OR heavy armour (choose one)
2. **Offering of Blood:** Access Reroll Pool for melee attack and damage rolls

**Common Phrases:**

**Common Icons:** Slender crescent moon forming a circle; Giant spider or webs; Cracked mirror

| 2 | Invoke trickery and guile |
| 3 | Delve the dark places of the world |
| 4 | Embrace mystery and wonder |
| 5 | Consort with mad and un-mad alike |
| 6 | Find comfort in the night |

#### Patron Benefits

### 6. Wodon
- **Domains:** Knowledge, art, wisdom and justice
- **Depicted:** Giant owl OR wizened human with two heads (one male, one female) leaning on runed staff or stack of tomes
- **Related Activities:** Seeking/giving advice, study, investigation, meditation, artistic endeavours, justice
- **Feast Day:** Reverie

**Common Phrases:**
- "Wodon guide you"
- "Knowledge is power"
- "One cannot hide from one's self"
- "Vengeance is a pit, justice a door"

**Common Icons:** Stack of tomes; Dual-headed bust; Giant owl with scales and/or hammer

#### Tenets
| Number | Tenet |
|--------|-------|
| 1 | Your word is your bond |
| 2 | See justice done |
| 3 | Learning is blessed; expand and strengthen your ken |
| 4 | Preserve the old lore and relics |
| 5 | Muse upon the wonderment of art |

#### Patron Benefits
1. Advantage on General Lore related checks
2. **Hammer of Judgment:** On successful melee hit, can spend Reroll die to knock target prone AND blind them until end of their next turn (no save, once per target)
3. Additional Spell-like Blessings:
   - Inescapable Unmasking
   - Whispers of the Watchers

## Designer's Notes

> ⚠️ **Important:** In original LFG, Magic User class encompassed all forms of magic. Cultist changes this by creating implicit divide between arcane and divine power (even though combined spell list remains).

GM Options:
- Some GMs might permit BOTH Cultists and Magic Users in game
- Others just one, or neither
- Some Unique Features (Divine Blessings, Spell Casting, Faustian Pact) provide further themed magic options
